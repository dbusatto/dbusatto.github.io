DAMIEN BUSATTO-GASTON

Research

I am a postdoc at Université Libre de Bruxelles (ULB), in the research group Formal Methods and Verification.

From September 2016 to December 2019, I was a PHD Student at Aix-Marseille Université (AMU), in the Laboratoire d'Informatique et Systemes (LIS), as a member of the team Modelisation and Verification (MOVE). I worked on Weighted Timed Games under the supervision of Benjamin Monmege and Pierre-Alain Reynier. See my CV (updated in 2016) and my PHD thesis.
